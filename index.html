What we do

UTOPIA​ , as google says, ​ an imagined place or state of things​ ​ in​ ​ which​ ​ everything​ ​ is​ ​ perfect​ .
In real world, yes nothing may seem perfect, but as humans we strive for it. We build our own versions of utopia. We dream on it, We believe in it and we work hard enough to achieve it. Utopia may not be a destination but it is the direction on which every human being​ ​ moves.
Science and Art naturally overlap. Both are a means of investigation. Both involve ideas, theories, and hypotheses that are tested in places where mind and hand come together—the laboratory and studio. Artists or scientists study—materials, people, culture, history, religion, mythology— and learn to transform information into​ ​ something​ ​ else.
Here in ​ The UTOPIA Academy ​ we intend to bring them together, on a common ground. To create a collaborative platform which helps the future generation to make the best​ ​ out​ ​ of​ ​ there​ ​ own​ ​ personal​ ​ interests.
